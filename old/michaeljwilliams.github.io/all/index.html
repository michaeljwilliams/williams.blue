Michael J. Williams is a designer, developer, and user experience activist. He loves sharing his ideas and his work, learning new things, and writing about himself in the third person.

Interesting fact: Michael's most prized possession is his blowtorch. Blowtorch = fun.

All joking aside, my dream and calling is to make the web—and the world—a better place.
